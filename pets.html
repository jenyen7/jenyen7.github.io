Bengal Cat

The Bengal cat is a cat breed from the leopard cat and other domestic cats.


Health

Bengal cats have several genetic diseases. One of the most common genetic disorder is Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy, so called HCM.
The disease will cause the heart muscle to be abnormally thick, reducing its capacity to pump blood.

Care

We can take care of bengal cats like we take care of other dometic cats. It is suggested to put more cat trees in the house because these cats really love to climb.
